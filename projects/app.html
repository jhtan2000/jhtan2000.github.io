Storia (App)

[Group Project] UI, App, Figma, Android Studio

Storia is a learning-based interactive storytelling application. This application offers users a variety of fairy tales not typically found in markets. By making different decisions, users can decide how the story ends.

I'm in charge of designing the application's wireframe, prototype, and developing front-end. For database storage, I used SQL.
