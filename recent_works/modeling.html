#3Ds Max #Individual Work

Poster

The poster's main objective is to encourage the audience to take a break during the busy assignment and project so that they can continue with more energy. The target users for this poster is the students who are busy with assignments and projects.

This poster shows a student's rooms. He works on modelling 3D objects from day to night, using only a computer and a light bulb for illumination. He continues to work, and the object he is modelling is transformed into the real object on the monitor screen., the clock is ticking over to next year now, even though the bulb is tired. Suddenly, a few words appeared, it was an AI reminds him that it was time to take a break.
