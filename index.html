Lacquer thread sculpture

[Group] Documentary Video - Premiere Pro

Lacquer thread sculpture is the heritage of ancient Buddhist sculpture art, it was inspired by the lacquer thread technology of Song and Yuan dynasties, especially the Drain powder and clay line carving skills.

In this documentary video, my groupmate visited Master Chen Wen Long in Hongtang, Xike Town, Tong 'an District, Xiamen city to closely learn more about lacquer thread sculpture culture.

I was in responsible of processing the raw video footage. My editing portion begins at 4:18, after which I hand it off to my groupmate for post-processing and subtitles.
